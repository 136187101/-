1. desc 命令 
格式: desc tablename columnname 

例子: 
desc `table` `mid` 
desc `table` '%abc%' 

2. show columns 命令 
格式: show columns from tablename like columnname 
例子: 
show columns from `table` like 'mid' 
show columns from `table` like '%abc%' 

3. describe 命令 
格式: describe tablename columnname 
describe 相于 show columns from 
例子: 
describe `table` `mid` 

describe `table` '%abc%'
